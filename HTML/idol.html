Early life and Education

Nay Toe was born on 9 September 1981 in Taw Hthu Village in Manaung Township, Cheduba Island, . He began his career as a model and eventually transitioned to cinema. He graduated from Yangon University with a BSc in Mathematics.

Career

A three-time academy award winner Nay Toe is among the industry's biggest names. He is known and worshiped by his natual talent. As such he is amoung the coutry's most highly paid actors, earining up to USD30,000 for a film. He is also famous for crossdressing dancing on state together with other well-known actors. They call themselves the Htawara Hnoinsi (Eternal Rose), and pervform at fundraising concerts for commuunities in need.
